Policies

Eligibility Requirements

Applicants must be U.S. citizens or permanent residents over 18 and meet financial or organizational criteria.

Application Deadlines

All applications must be submitted before the posted deadline. Late entries will not be reviewed.

Data Privacy

All personal and financial information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without consent.

Grant Usage Terms

Funds must be used strictly for approved purposes such as education, business development, or housing support.

Review Timeline

Applications are typically reviewed within 2-4 weeks. Applicants will be notified via email.

Disbursement Process

Approved grants are disbursed via direct deposit or mailed check within 10 business days of approval.

Support & Contact

Our support team is available Monday–Friday to assist with questions about your application or eligibility.
