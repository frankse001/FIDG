About the Federal Innovation & Development Grant (FIDG)

The federal Innovation & Development Grant (FIDG) is a landmark initiative by the Ministry of Economic Development, designed to catalyze significant advancements in key sectors vital to national progress. This grant aims to provide substantial financial support and strategic resources to innovative projects, ambitious entrepreneurs, non-profit organizations, and community-led initiatives that demonstrate a clear potential for transformative impact. Our vision is to foster a vibrant ecosystem of creativity, problem-solving, and sustainable development that resonates across all facets of society. The NIDG seeks to address critical national challenges while simultaneously creating new opportunities for economic prosperity and social well-being.

  • Startups and Small Businesses: Businesses with innovative solutions in technology, green energy, agriculture, healthcare, and education.
  • Non-Profit Organizations: Registered charities and NGOs engaged in community development, social welfare, environmental protection, and cultural preservation.
  • Academic and Research Institutions: Teams or individuals conducting cutting-edge research with practical applications that benefit the public.
  • Individual Innovators & Entrepreneurs: Visionary individuals with groundbreaking ideas that require seed funding to become a reality.

Application Process & Key Dates:

The application process is designed to be streamlined and transparent, ensuring all eligible applicants have a fair chance.

Grant Benefits:

  • Financial Support: Grants ranging from $50,000 to $500,000, tailored to project scope and impact.
  • Mentorship & Training: Access to a network of industry experts, business coaches, and specialized workshops.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with potential investors, collaborators, and government officials.
  • Visibility & Recognition: Showcase your project on national platforms, gaining invaluable exposure.
  • Technical Assistance: Support for project planning, implementation, and impact measurement.

We encourage all prospective applicants to thoroughly read the official grant guidelines available on our website before applying. Successful applications will clearly articulate their project's objectives, methodology, expected outcomes, and a robust plan for sustainability beyond the grant period.
